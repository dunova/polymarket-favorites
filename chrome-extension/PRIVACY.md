# Privacy Policy for Polymarket Assistant

**Last Updated: January 5, 2026**

## Overview

Polymarket Assistant is a browser extension that helps users analyze and track markets on Polymarket. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Market favorites**: Saved locally in your browser
- **Analysis preferences**: Stored locally

### What We DON'T Collect
- Personal information
- Wallet addresses
- Trading history
- Authentication tokens
- Cookies from Polymarket

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

This extension accesses the following Polymarket APIs:
- `gamma-api.polymarket.com` - For market data
- `clob.polymarket.com` - For price information

These requests are made directly from your browser to Polymarket's servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your data
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your browsing activity

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all extension data via Chrome settings
- Uninstall the extension at any time
- Export your favorites (feature coming soon)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be noted with a new "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store.
